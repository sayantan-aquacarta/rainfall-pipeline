Reservoir Storage Monitor

The Central Water Commission (CWC) publishes weekly live storage data for India's 150+ major reservoirs, covering ~257 BCM of total capacity. This module will track live storage as a percentage of Full Reservoir Level (FRL), compare against last year and the last 10-year average, and correlate upstream rainfall from this pipeline with downstream storage response — providing the water-food-energy nexus view missing from rainfall data alone.
