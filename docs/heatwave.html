Heat & Cold Wave Alert Monitor

IMD issues daily district-level weather warnings classified on a four-colour scale: Red (take action), Orange (be prepared), Yellow (be aware), and Green (no warning). This dashboard will track alert distributions, multi-day heat and cold wave episodes, and show the alert history for any district — enabling early-warning analysis at scale.

IMD Warning Classification
Red — Take Action (Severe)
Orange — Be Prepared (High)
Yellow — Be Aware (Moderate)
Green — No Warning

IMD issues these warnings for extreme heat, cold waves, thunderstorms, cyclones, heavy rainfall, and fog. This module focuses on heat wave and cold wave classifications specifically, correlating them with the temperature data from the Temperature Extremes module.
