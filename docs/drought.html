Methodology: SPI computed using a parameterised gamma distribution (shape α = 1/CV² ≈ 9.77; scale β = LPA/α; CV = 0.32 from Parthasarathy et al., 1987). The LPA baseline comes from the IMD "normal" column in the daily rainfall PDF. As the archive grows to ≥ 10 same-season-day observations, the gamma is re-fitted from observed data. Reference: WMO-No. 1090 (McKee et al., 1993).

Data source: India Meteorological Department · SPI: WMO-No. 1090 · Open-source pipeline
